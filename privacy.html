Privacy Policy for True Price Calculator for MAC.BID

Last updated: June 14, 2026

True Price Calculator for MAC.BID is a Chrome extension that estimates the total cost of MAC.BID auction items by adding buyer fees, lot fees, and sales tax to visible auction prices on MAC.BID pages.

Data Collection

The extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data. It does not collect personally identifiable information, financial information, authentication information, personal communications, location, web history, user activity, or website content.

Local Use of Page Information

The extension reads visible auction information on MAC.BID pages, such as current bid amounts and item price details, only inside the user's browser to calculate and display estimated totals. This information is not sent to the developer or to any third party.

Stored Preferences

The extension may save calculator preferences, such as fee settings, tax settings, and display options, using Chrome extension storage so those settings persist between sessions. These preferences are used only to provide the extension's calculator features.

Third Parties

The extension does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties. It does not use user data for advertising, analytics, creditworthiness, or lending purposes.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer through the support information listed on the Chrome Web Store item page.
